Threat number nine: Human-Agent Trust Exploitation.

In the 2025 L L M Top Ten this was misinformation — a plausible but wrong output treated as fact by a human or downstream system.

With agents the stakes jump. Users no longer just read the wrong answer, they approve the wrong action. Agents speak in fluent, confident, professional tone, and users rubber-stamp approvals on work they have not actually verified. Social engineering is now agent-to-human, at scale.

My top mitigation is Azure A I Foundry Evaluations. Automated relevance, coherence and completeness scoring catches plausible-but-wrong answers before your users learn to over-trust them.

Pair that with Copilot Studio human-in-the-loop. Built-in approval flows make sensitive actions pause for explicit confirmation.
